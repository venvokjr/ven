About Me

I'm a Tanzanian software engineer specializing in building practical solutions for local challenges. My passion lies in creating software that simplifies everyday problems for Tanzanians.

I developed FMSPoint (fmspoint.com) to revolutionize financial management for community events after witnessing the struggles organizers face during fundraising activities like harambees, weddings, and other social gatherings.

With expertise in multiple programming languages and a deep understanding of local needs, I bridge the gap between technology and practical solutions for Tanzanian communities.
